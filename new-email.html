Hands-On Exposure to Real-World Digital Forensics

Get ready to tackle real-world cybersecurity challenges with back-to-back sessions focusing on combating misinformation and falsification attacks. These threats involve deliberate manipulation of data, communications, or transactions to deceive and disrupt digital systems.

Open to everyone, from students to professionals, this event provides practical insights into detecting and mitigating such attacks. With four immersive sessions running consecutively, you'll gain hands-on experience, explore real-world case studies, and learn tools and techniques to safeguard against cyber threats.

Join us to sharpen your digital forensics skills and play a key role in combating misinformation in the digital age.

Quiz Round: The Hunt for Real vs. Fake (Hosted on Unstop)

Welcome to the ultimate challenge where your skills in detecting AI-altered content will be put to the test! This exciting quiz round will take place on Unstop, where participants will battle it out to distinguish between real and manipulated content. Here’s what you need to know about the event:

Round Breakdown

AI-Altered Text (20 Minutes)

In this segment, you will analyze a variety of text samples and identify which ones have been generated or modified by AI tools. Be prepared for some mind-bending examples that test your instincts and critical thinking skills!

Fake Audio Detection (50 Minutes)

You’ll listen to audio clips and determine which ones have been manipulated using AI. From deepfake voices to subtle edits, this segment will challenge your auditory perception and ability to discern between the real and the fake.

Fake Image Detection (1 Hour 30 Minutes)

In this final segment, you’ll analyze a series of images and identify the ones that have been altered or generated by AI. Look closely for clues in pixel patterns, lighting inconsistencies, and other subtle hints that might reveal the truth.
